Zoom Meeting

Zoom is an online meeting platform for our weekly lessons where tutor will share their screen while teaching and students need to listen, turn on camera and ask questions when you need

How to use
Zoom

Step 1

Search for Zoom on Google or click the link below and download it on your desktop

Choose
Zoom Workplace desktop app
https://zoom.us/download

Step 2

Sign up using your email and login to start using Zoom

Step 3

Go to your Google Classroom and click on the permanent Zoom link to join the weekly lesson

Step 4

After you joined the class, you will see this interface. Refer to the image for info

1. Audio: To turn on/off your microphone.
2. Video: To turn on/off your camera.
3. Chat: To chat in the lesson if you have any issues with your microphone.
4. Share: To share your laptop/desktop screen if you want to show your works or if you occur any issues.
