Google Classroom

Here is the place where you can see your assignments, notes, announcement, and recording of your class that will be publish by your tutor after class has ended every week. You also need to submit your assignments in Google Classroom. Let's see how to use it!

How to use
Google Classroom

Step 1

Download and open Google Chrome

Step 2

Click the grid button beside your profile photo. Scroll down until you see
"Google Classroom"

If you can't see Google Classroom, just search for it

Step 3

You will see the home screen of your class in Google Classroom. Click on your class to see the contents and assignments from your class.

Link to join the Google Classroom has been sent directly to your WhatsApp and email. Click on the link to join.

Step 4

After you entered your Classroom, we call this tab as "Stream" where you can see the overview of your assignments, notes, announcements and class recordings when you scroll down.

See at the top section, there are 3 tabs

1.  Stream
2. Classwork
3. People

Step 5

In the "Classwork tab", you can see all of your assignment and you can submit your assignments by clicking on the task in your classwork

Step 6 - Submit Assignment

Open your task

1. click "Add or create" to add your assignment file

2. Choose your file and upload

Step 7 - Submit Assignment

After your file was uploaded, click "Hand in" to submit

Step 8

In the "People tab", you can see the list of your classmates and tutor
