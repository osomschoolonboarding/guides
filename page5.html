TeamViewer

TeamViewer is an all-in-one solution for remote support, remote access, and online meetings. It allows us to assist you remotely by controlling your device if you have any technical issues to install software or setup your software after installation

How to use
TeamViewer

Step 1

Search for TeamViewer on Google or click the link below and download it on your desktop

Choose
TeamViewer full client (64-bit)
https://www.teamviewer.com

Step 2

After finish install. Sign up and login to your TeamViewer

Go to "Remote Control" section and you will see Your ID and Password. Share your ID and Password to admin for us to be able to control your device. Don't worry about your privacy, we will not access any other folders and data on your device. The password also will be automatically reset every time we disconnected from your device.
